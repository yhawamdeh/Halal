    Get Certified

    Most Americans eat some halal food every day, but chances are they’re not aware of it. Take a walk down the aisles of any supermarket and you will see that certification appears on over 60% of America’s produced foods that are certified kosher, from the coveted Oreo to the thirst-quenching Coca-Cola. Over $150 billion of halal certified products are consumed annually, and spending continues to rise dramatically.
